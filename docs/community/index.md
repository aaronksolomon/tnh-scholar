# Community & Outreach

This section contains documentation for community partners, stakeholders, and collaborators who support the TNH Scholar project within the Plum Village tradition.

## Contents

- [Parallax Press & Plum Village Editorial Overview](parallax-overview.md) - Project introduction for editorial and publishing stakeholders

## Purpose

These documents provide accessible, non-technical introductions to the TNH Scholar project for:

- **Parallax Press** and publishing partners
- **Plum Village Editorial** community members
- **Monastic communities** interested in the project's vision
- **Practitioners** exploring collaboration opportunities
- **Potential funders** and supporters

## Guiding Principles

In the spirit of Thầy's teachings, our community engagement emphasizes:

- **Clarity and accessibility** - Technical concepts explained with mindful language
- **Transparency** - Open sharing of goals, progress, and challenges
- **Collaboration** - Welcoming input and partnership from the community
- **Respect for lineage** - Honoring the Plum Village tradition and Thầy's teachings
- **Interbeing** - Recognizing the interconnected nature of all contributions
